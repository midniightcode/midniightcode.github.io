ABOUT CONTACT Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions I receive regarding my workflow, pricing, and availability.

What's the easiest way to contact you?

The easiest and fastest way to reach me is either through the Contact Form on my website or via direct message on Instagram. I typically reply within 24-48 hours!

How long does it take you to finish an art piece?

It heavily depends on the complexity of the piece. A simple character sketch might take 2-4 hours, while a fully rendered illustration with background can take anywhere from 10 to 30+ hours spread over a couple of weeks.

What are your prices?

My prices vary based on the project's scope, usage rights (commercial vs personal), and level of detail. Please reach out via the contact form with your project details, and I can provide a personalized quote!

Do you do collabs?

Yes, I'm always open to creative collaborations, especially with other artists or developers! Reach out to me with your idea, and let's see if we're a good fit.

Do you take on web development freelance work?

Absolutely! I offer UI/UX design and front-end development services. Whether you need a landing page, a portfolio, or a complete web app interface, we can discuss the details.
