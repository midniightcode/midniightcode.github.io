About Me

Hello! I am a digital artist and UX/UI web developer based in Texas. I specialize in the artistic aspect of digital creation, blending vivid storytelling with functional design. My journey began with a deep love for traditional sketching and painting, which eventually naturally evolved into a passion for digital mediums and interactive experiences.

My background in traditional art has heavily shaped my approach to digital design. I believe that every interface should tell a story and every illustration should evoke an emotion. By bridging the gap between aesthetic beauty and intuitive functionality, I aim to craft experiences that are not only visually stunning but also highly usable and accessible for everyone.

When I'm not designing wireframes or coding responsive layouts, you can usually find me experimenting with new character concepts, studying lighting and color theory, or exploring the latest trends in front-end development. I'm always looking for the next creative challenge that pushes my boundaries!
